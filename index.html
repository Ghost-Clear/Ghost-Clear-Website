Improve. Efficiently.

To enhance your ghosting experience, we have created a group of six devices that seamlessly connect to the Ghost Clear App. From the app you can launch a number of workouts that are all highly customizable. The six devices were meticulously crafted for ruggedness, ease of use, and longevity.

Workouts. Many options, your choice.

The 4 different workouts that you can choose from are the Timed Workout, the Number workout, the Beep test workout, and the Play a Pro workout. Each one can be programed to the intensity of your choice. During the workout, the app and the devices will indicate which corner to ghost to using the LEDs on the devices and speaker on the phone.

Goals. Set then achieve.

In the app, you are able to set goals that you would like to achieve. The View Your Goals screen allow you to closely track your goals and show you which ones have been achieved and which ones have not. As you complete each workout, the app automatically updates each goal's completion status.

Statistics. Everything is tracked.

The app provides detailed statistics of the workouts you have completed. These statistics include weekly workout progress, beep test progress, workout composition and overall ghosting stats.

Devices. Connect and train.

The 6 ghosting devices are placed at the 4 corners and the 2 sides of the squash court. When starting a workout, the devices will automatically connect to your phone via bluetooth so you do not need to go through the hassle of connecting each of them through settings. During the workout, the devices will light up to indicate which corner you need to ghost to and will detect your body when you approach them.
