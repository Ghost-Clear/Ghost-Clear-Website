About Us

To enhance your ghosting experience, we have created a group of six devices that seamlessly connect to the Ghost Clear App. From the app you can launch a number of workouts that are all highly customizable.


The six devices were meticulously crafted for ruggedness, ease of use, and longevity. The devices are used to indicate which corner to ghost to and to detect a swing of a racket.

Workouts

There are 4 available workouts to choose from. A timed workout lets you choose a certain amount of time to ghost per set. A number workout lets you choose a certain amount of ghosts per set. A beep test workout makes you ghost sets of 6 ghosts for as long as you can while the time to complete each set slowly decreases. The play a pro workout allows you to simulate a real pro match and allows you to choose the player you want to simulate and the difficulty.
